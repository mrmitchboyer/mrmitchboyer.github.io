Hi, I'm Mitch Boyer. I make picture books and really love pizza.

Including my childhood, I've lived in 9 states, 15 cities, and 22 different houses or apartments. Let's just say I'm pretty good at moving. Other things I'm good at? Storytelling, photography, and eating too much pizza (Paulie Gee's is my favorite).

Right now, I'm working on a book about a giant dachshund: Vivian the Dog Moves to the Big City (HarperCollins, 2017).

I also make photo illustrations, motion graphics videos, and geometric illustrations for cool people and companies.
